About Us

AgriFlow is dedicated to modernizing agriculture through innovative technology. Our mission is to improve efficiency and sustainability in farming practices by leveraging data-driven insights and precision agriculture techniques.

Challenges

In modern agriculture, the challenge lies in efficiently using resources while ensuring sustainable crop production. Traditional farming methods often struggle to adapt to changing environmental conditions, leading to inefficiencies and environmental harm. Our challenge is to develop innovative solutions that help farmers maximize yields while minimizing resource use and environmental impact. Precision agriculture emerges as our solution, offering a data-driven approach to optimize farming practices and address these challenges.

Objectives

Our goals involve creating a Smart Farming Decision Support System to transform agricultural practices. .

  • Efficient Water Level Control: Monitor and control tank water levels using sensors and a solenoid valve to prevent overflow and optimize waterusage.
  • Remote Monitoring and Management: Enable remote access to water level data and system control via an OLED display for convenient monitoring and management from anywhere.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Design an intuitive interface with clear visual feedback on water levels and system status to simplify user interaction and enhance usability.

What We Do

We're building a powerful system that gives farmers real-time insights into their fields and crops. Using automated irrigation, remote control, and easy-to-understand data visuals, we empower farmers to make smart decisions for better yields and environmental stewardship.
